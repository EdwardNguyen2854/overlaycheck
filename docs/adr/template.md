# ADR Template

```md
# ADR-XXX: Decision Title

## Status

Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-YYY

## Context

What forced this decision? What alternatives were considered?

## Decision

What was chosen and why?

## Consequences

- **Positive**: what becomes easier or better
- **Negative**: what becomes harder or more complex
- **Neutral**: what doesn't change much
```

Replace `XXX` with the next available number.
